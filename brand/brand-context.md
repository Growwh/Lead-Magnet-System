# Brand Context

> Replace this with your complete brand design system. See the config.yaml for the structure.

## What to Include

1. **Who you are** - Name, role, what you do, who you serve
2. **Your ICP** - Ideal customer profile, their pain points, what they want
3. **Color palette** - Core colors, secondary colors, neutral scale, dark mode map
4. **Typography** - Font stack, type scale, rules
5. **Visual style** - Neumorphism tokens, shadows, glass panels, corner radius, spacing
6. **Components** - Buttons, cards, inputs, tags, progress bars
7. **Do's and Don'ts** - Hard rules for visual consistency

See the example in the repository for a complete brand-context.md template.
