# Voice Context

> Replace this with your complete voice and personality guide.

## What to Include

1. **How you talk** - Speech patterns, verbal tics, common phrases
2. **How you write** - Professional vs. personal modes, sentence structure
3. **Writing rules** - Banned words, banned patterns, required patterns
4. **Voice examples** - "Sounds like you" vs. "Does NOT sound like you"
5. **Content structure** - How to structure lead magnets (opening, body, CTA)
6. **The checklist** - Pre-publish quality checks

See the example in the repository for a complete voice-context.md template.
